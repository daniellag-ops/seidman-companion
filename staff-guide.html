Sending Melody
to a patient
Melody is a personal AI companion for patients going through IVF. Each patient receives their own private link — it knows their treatment phase, answers their questions in real time, and supports them between appointments.
How to generate a link
Three steps
Takes under a minute. Each link is unique to that patient.
🔗 seidman-ai.com/admin
1
Go to seidman-ai.com/admin and enter the admin password.
2
Type the patient's full name and click Generate link.
3
Copy the link and send it to the patient via WhatsApp.
Suggested WhatsApp message
What to send the patient
You can copy and paste the message below, with the link added at the end.
שלום,
מצורף הקישור האישי שלך ל-Melody — הליווי הדיגיטלי של פרופ׳ זיידמן לאורך הטיפול.

דרך הקישור תוכלי לשאול שאלות, לקרוא טיפים מותאמים לשלב שלך, ולקבל מידע בין הפגישות.

🔗 [הכניסי כאן את הקישור שיצרת]
What happens next
The patient's experience
When the patient opens their link for the first time, they choose their current treatment phase. From there they can ask questions, read personalised wellness tips, and save notes — all in Hebrew or English. The link is private and works only for them.
There is also a private doctor's dashboard available to Prof. Seidman for monitoring patient activity across the clinic.
